How I became interested in Game Development and Computer Science

My two older brohters introduced me to gaming pretty early. The older one of them had a PlayStation and the middle one a GameBoy. And if they weren't playing I was allowed to. Later on we got a Computer and me and my middle brother played a MMORPG named Metin2 on it. As the days passed we spend more and more time playing and grinding in Metin2. After some time I stumbled upon a online board about Metin2.

The main sections on the board were about private Metin2 servers and cheats. I started to follow tutorials about running my own private Metin2 server over LogMeIn Hamachi, unpacking game files and modding the game. To be honest I didn't know exactly what I was doing besides following instructions but I sure had a lot of fun.

You needed a website where people could register for you private server, thus you would also find a lot of premade template websites there you could download and use after changing some variables. This was my first expierence with coding and webdesign.

I started teaching myself very basic HTML, CSS and later on basic PHP. I tasted blood, so most of my afternoons during middle school were spend this way. All of this was more of a hobby until I finished playing my first AAA video game on the PC, Assassin's Creed Revelations. It was the straw that broke the camels back and from that moment on I pursued my dream to become a Game Developer.
