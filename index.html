About Me

My two older brohters introduced me to gaming pretty early. The older one of them had a PlayStation and the middle one a GameBoy. And if they weren't playing I was allowed to. Later on we got a Computer and me and my middle brother played a MMORPG named Metin2 on it. As the days passed we spend more and more time playing and grinding in Metin2. Video games have had a strong influence on my life: as a child, i spend a lot of my spare time with them and later my professional career were strongly influenced by my love for video games.
